City Lights

Commercial • Completed: 2023 • Client: UrbanX

Objective

To create a visually compelling commercial video that highlights the vibrant nightlife and urban energy of the city, appealing to young professionals.

Constraints

Limited shooting time during golden hour, tight client deadlines, and the need to maintain brand consistency.

Editorial Choices

Used dynamic pacing with quick cuts, warm color grading to enhance the golden hour feel, and incorporated subtle motion graphics to emphasize key moments.

Results

The video increased client engagement by 40% and was featured in multiple online campaigns, receiving positive feedback for its cinematic quality.

"The editing brought our vision to life with unmatched clarity and emotion."

Deliverables

  • 1080p full-length cut
  • 30-second social media cutdown
  • 9:16 vertical reels package
